Vaccination Services

Vaccination is considered one of the most important public health interventions in history, saving millions of lives and preventing countless cases of illness and disability. However, vaccine hesitancy and misinformation have become significant challenges in recent years, leading to outbreaks of vaccine-preventable diseases in some areas. It is important for individuals to seek accurate information about vaccines from reliable sources and to make informed decisions about their own vaccination status.
